Meet Our Team

Discover the passionate individuals who make TOA Cares' mission possible. Our diverse teams work collaboratively to create lasting change and defend the rights of children and families worldwide.

Events Team

Our Events Team orchestrates meaningful gatherings, fundraisers, and community engagement activities that bring people together for our cause. From large-scale galas to intimate community meetings, they create memorable experiences that inspire action and support.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Planning and executing fundraising events
  • Coordinating community engagement activities
  • Managing event logistics and vendor relationships
  • Creating memorable experiences for supporters

Business Partnerships & Outreach (BPH)

The BPH Team builds strategic partnerships with corporations, organizations, and community leaders to expand our reach and impact. They cultivate relationships that create sustainable support for our programs and initiatives.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Developing corporate partnership strategies
  • Managing sponsor relationships
  • Creating mutually beneficial collaboration opportunities
  • Expanding organizational network and influence

Design Team

Our creative Design Team brings our mission to life through compelling visuals, user-friendly interfaces, and impactful marketing materials. They ensure our message resonates across all platforms and touchpoints.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Creating visual brand identity and materials
  • Designing user-friendly digital experiences
  • Developing campaign graphics and illustrations
  • Maintaining consistent visual communication

Drama Team

The Drama Team uses the power of storytelling and performance to educate, inspire, and raise awareness about critical issues affecting children and families. Through creative presentations, they make complex topics accessible and engaging.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Developing educational performances and presentations
  • Creating engaging storytelling experiences
  • Facilitating workshops and interactive sessions
  • Using arts to communicate important messages

Floor Director Team

The Floor Director Team ensures smooth operations during events, meetings, and activities. They coordinate logistics, manage schedules, and provide on-site leadership to guarantee successful execution of all programs.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Coordinating event and meeting logistics
  • Managing on-site operations and flow
  • Ensuring safety and security protocols
  • Facilitating smooth program execution

Fundraising Team

Our dedicated Fundraising Team develops and implements strategies to secure the financial resources needed to support our programs. They work tirelessly to ensure sustainable funding for our mission.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Developing comprehensive fundraising strategies
  • Managing donor relationships and stewardship
  • Creating compelling fundraising campaigns
  • Securing grants and major gifts

Games Team

The Games Team creates engaging, interactive experiences that educate while entertaining. They develop games and activities that help communicate our message in fun, accessible ways that resonate with diverse audiences.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Designing educational games and activities
  • Creating interactive learning experiences
  • Developing youth engagement programs
  • Making learning fun and accessible

Gift Team

The Gift Team manages in-kind donations, volunteer appreciation, and special recognition programs. They ensure that contributions are properly acknowledged and that supporters feel valued for their generosity.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Managing in-kind donation programs
  • Coordinating volunteer recognition initiatives
  • Organizing appreciation events and activities
  • Ensuring proper acknowledgment of contributions

Consumption Team

The Consumption Team handles food services, catering coordination, and hospitality for events and meetings. They ensure that nutritional needs are met while creating welcoming environments for all participants.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Planning and coordinating event catering
  • Managing food service logistics
  • Ensuring dietary accommodations are met
  • Creating welcoming hospitality experiences

MC (Master of Ceremonies) Team

The MC Team provides professional hosting and facilitation for events, ensuring engaging presentations and smooth program flow. They are the voice that guides audiences through meaningful experiences.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Hosting and facilitating events and programs
  • Engaging audiences with compelling presentations
  • Managing event timing and program flow
  • Creating dynamic and memorable experiences

Multimedia Team

The Multimedia Team creates powerful video content, manages live streaming, and produces digital media that amplifies our message. They capture and share the stories that inspire action and change.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Producing video content and documentaries
  • Managing live streaming and broadcast services
  • Creating digital media campaigns
  • Capturing and sharing impactful stories

Publication Team

The Publication Team develops written content, manages communications, and ensures our message reaches audiences through various written formats. They are responsible for clear, compelling, and consistent messaging.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Creating newsletters, reports, and publications
  • Managing written communications and messaging
  • Developing content strategies and calendars
  • Ensuring consistent voice and brand messaging
