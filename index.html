Our Mission

Empowering communities through education, advocacy, and direct support to create lasting positive change in the lives of those who need it most.

Our Work

Across communities worldwide, TOA Cares defends the rights of children and families. We work to ensure that every child has access to education, healthcare, and the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our approach combines grassroots advocacy with strategic initiatives to create sustainable change in education, healthcare, and social justice for the most vulnerable populations.

How We Help

Discover the ways that TOA Cares advocates for the rights of children and families around the world to protect and empower every community, every day.

Education

Ensuring every child has access to quality education, from early childhood development to vocational training and life skills.

Child Protection

Protecting children from violence, exploitation, and abuse while promoting their rights and well-being in every community.

Health & Nutrition

Providing essential healthcare services, immunizations, and nutrition programs to ensure children survive and thrive.

Emergency Response

Delivering life-saving aid and support to children and families affected by conflicts, natural disasters, and humanitarian crises.

Water & Sanitation

Providing access to clean water, safe sanitation, and hygiene education to prevent disease and promote healthy communities.

Advocacy & Rights

Advocating for policy changes and working with governments to uphold and advance children's rights in all areas of life.
